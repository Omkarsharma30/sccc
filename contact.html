Frequently Asked Questions

What are your office hours?

Our office is open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We recommend calling before visiting to ensure availability.

How can I verify my certificate?

You can verify your certificate by visiting our Results page and using the certificate verification tool, or by contacting our office directly with your certificate number.

Yes, we provide job placement assistance to our students after successful completion of their courses. We have tie-ups with various companies and organizations.
